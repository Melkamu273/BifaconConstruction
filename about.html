Our Story

Bifacon Engineering is one of the leading Grade One Contractor. We actively involved actively involved building, water work, and Electro Mechanical work and road construction projects all over the country. We have completed a number of contracts with various multinational and local companies and individuals. Our Moto is “We believe in quality not quantity”. We are experienced in providing quality services and products. We provides assistance to clients with designs, construction, and logistics. With new engineering developments and changes in the field of architecture and construction, the demand for these services is increasing. This national presence offers our clients complete designs, engineering, and material supply resources that are vertically integrated with engineering solutions to optimize their operation by lowering their costs and reducing their time to market. With the need for construction growing at an exponential rate, we are prepared to work with our partners and subcontractors to meet the challenges of the future.

Security & Safety

The manpower is our most valuable resource. To ensure a safe workplace and to protect clients’ investment and facilities, the Group has evolved a strong health and safety culture driven by a goal to achieve “zero accidents” and “zero man-hours lost”. The safety culture has been instilled with the conviction that every accident is preventable. The company fully acknowledges that by complying with stringent Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) procedures, zero accidents can be accomplished.
